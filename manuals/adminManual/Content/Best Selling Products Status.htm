Best Selling Products

Using Best Selling Products, Admin can easily understand the list of top 10 products which are moving very fast in market. The top 10 products that are listed based on the number of quantities that are sold. So it is basically, the maximum number of quantity of products which are moving fast in market. The graph will be shown in descending order that is from maximum to minimum number of quantities sold. Using this Admin can get an idea of only the top 10 products moving in market.

You can not customize the graph based on criteria. By default, the top 10 products will be listed.

 

  1. Click Business Intelligence > Store Status > Best Selling Products will open Best Selling Products page.
  2. The graph will be shown based on the number of quantity of product moving in market. In X - axis the names on top 10 products will be taken and in Y - axis the number of products that are sold in market will be taken.
