Product Reviews

When user has reviewed a product in the site, ZeusCart 2.3 will not make it active immediately. It just displays "Your review has been accepted for moderation" message. Then the reviewed product will be displayed in the Product Review page of Admin Panel. Admin can analyze the review and make it active in single click. Admin has the facility to add related products for the selected product using this module too.

 

  1. Click Catalog > Products > Product Reviews will open Product Review Details page.
    • The list all products reviewed by user will be displayed.
    • The Products are displayed with the information of User Name, Product Name, Review Summary, Review, and Posted Date.

 

 

  1. Search: The number of users may review on all products and many users may review on the same product so all the reviewed products will be displayed in Product Review Details page. If you want to search particular product, enter the keyword that you know in the respective text box provided above the corresponding field.
    • Click "Search" button will list the information of products based on entered keyword.
  2. If you need to activate the product review to be displayed in the site, then click link and it shows "Successfully Activated" message.
  3. When you do not need to display Product Review in site, then click link which shows "Successfully Inactivated" message.
  4. Admin can click on the name of product to view entire information of product review and which opens PRODUCT DETAILS page.
  5. Export Report: ZeusCart provides facility to export the product review list in four different formats of Excel, XML, CSV, and TAB.
    • Click on the respective format you need to export the report and click "Export" button, then you can download the file.

Suppose if you need to export the review list in Excel format, and then select Excel in the list box provided, then click "Export" button. It will open "Opening Product_Review.xls" dialog box, Click "OK" will download excel file otherwise click "Cancel" to exit from downloading the file.
