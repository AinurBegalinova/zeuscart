Best Selling Products

Using Best Selling Products, Admin can easily understand the list of top 10 products which are moving very fast in market. The top 10 products that are listed based on the number of quantities that are sold. So it is basically, the maximum number of quantity of products which are moving fast in market. The list of top 10 product details will be displayed in a grid. The list will be shown in descending order that is from maximum to minimum number of quantities sold. Using this Admin can get an idea of top 10 products moving in market.

 

  1. Click Others > Best Selling Products will open Best Selling Products page.
  2. The list of details like Product name, Category Name, Price per item and the number of items sold will be displayed.
