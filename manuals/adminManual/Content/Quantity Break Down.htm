Quantity Break Down

In Quantity Break Down module, Admin can understand the status of products that are moving based on the quantity selected by users. While ordering, a single user may order for one product or more than on products. So based on the order of products the graph will be shown. So the quantity of product will be taken with the percentage of moving. Admin can customize the product status based on the several criteria that for last 30 days, for current month, for last month, from the time when ZeusCart 2.3 installed and your own custom function based on the date.

Quantity Break Down is called as the number of percentage of users buying single product in a single checkout or the number of percentage of users buying two products in single checkout.

 

  1. Click Business Intelligence > Store Status > Quantity Break Down will open Quantity Break Down page.
  • By default, the graph will be displayed for the products that are ordered by the percentage of users from the current date till last 30 days.
  1. Date Range: Using this list box, you can view the graph based on your own options by selecting the criteria listed. Select the option and click GO button will display the graph based on selected criteria.
  • Last 30 Days: The number of products ordered per order for last 30 days (current date till last 30 days) will be displayed in a graph. In X - axis, the quantity of products moved in last 30 days will be taken and in Y - axis the percentage of users for last will be taken.
  • This Month: When you select this option then the number of products ordered per order in this month will be displayed. In X - axis, the quantity of products moved in current will be taken and in Y - axis the percentage of users will be taken.
  • Last Month: The number of products ordered per order in last month will be displayed. In X - axis, the quantity of products moved in last month will be taken and in Y - axis the percentage of users will be taken.

 

 

  • All Time: This is simply based on the number of products ordered per order from the time you have installed ZeusCart 2.3. In X - axis, the quantity of products moved from the time you have installed ZeusCart 2.3 will be taken and in Y - axis the percentage of users will be taken.
  • Custom: When you select this option, then the date fields will be displayed near by. Select the starting date and ending date between which you need to view about the number of products ordered per order. In X - axis, the quantity of products moved between the dates you have selected will be taken and in Y - axis the percentage of users will be taken.
