Inventory

Using Inventory module, Admin can view stock information and also can update the information of a product. Stock information means the remaining quantity of product. This module helps Admin to enter the available quantity and reorder level of the product. When the product got sold in the market, the stock quantity will be reduced and Admin can update the available quantity of product using this module.

 

  1. Click Catalog > Products > Inventory will open Product Inventory Management page.
    • The list of all products added currently in site will be displayed with information of Product Name, Re-Order Level, Stock on Hand and Status of product.

 

 

  1. Click icon provided in respective of product information to be added, will open Edit Product Inventory Page.

 

 

    • Product Name: The name of the product will be displayed.
    • Re-Order List: Enter the value of Re-Order Level for selected product which should be lesser than Stock on hand value. So that when the product started getting sold and when it reaches the entered Re-Order Level value than it will be displayed as Stock Low.
    • Stock on Hand: Admin should enter the number of quantity available currently for the product and which should be greater than the Re-Order level value. So that it will be displayed Yes for Availability of product in site.
    • Click Update Inventory will update the information for product.

We have provided Paging for viewing the inventory details. Click on the page numbers to go to corresponding pages and click Prev and Next to go back and to go next to the page where you are now. By default Ten inventory details will be listed in a page.
