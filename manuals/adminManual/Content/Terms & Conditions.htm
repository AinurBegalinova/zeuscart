Terms & Conditions

Using Terms & Conditions module, Admin can enter the detailed description of terms and conditions to be used when using the website. Admin can make an excellent content using WYSIWYG editor. It helps Admin to enter the content easy and effective.

 

  1. Click CMS > Terms & Conditions will open Terms & Conditions page.

 

 

  1. Enter the content to be displayed, when user clicks the Terms & Conditions link provided in footer of the site.
  2. Click Update Terms & Conditions button will display "Content Updated Successfully" message.
