Planning Considerations

Building a store involves a sequence of steps each of which require you to provide specific information. We therefore recommend that you collate all the information surrounding your business and the products that you intend to sell prior to commencing any work within ZeusCart 2.3.

 

  • To create Attributes, Click Catalog > Attributes > Attribute, will open Attribute page. You can follow the steps available in Attribute chapter to create the attributes and attribute values successful.
  • To create Categories and Sub Categories, Click Catalog > Categories > Category will open Main Category List page. You can follow the steps available in Category chapter to create Categories and Sub Categories successful.
  • You can check the payment settings using Settings > Payments Settings. You can enable the payment process you need.
