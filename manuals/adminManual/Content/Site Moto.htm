Site Moto

ZeusCart provides you separate module to add the name of Site Moto. The added name in site moto will be displayed in the title bar of website.

 

  1. Click Settings > Site Settings > Site Moto will open Site Moto page.

 

 

  1. Site Moto: Enter the name of moto be displayed in the site.
  2. Click Submit button will display "Site Moto Successfully Changed to <name of the added site moto>" message.
