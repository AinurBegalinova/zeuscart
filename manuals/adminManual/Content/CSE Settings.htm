CSE Settings

To compare the prices of products added in one site with other website product prices, the website called Price Runner is used. So Admin should register with the price runner website and he will be provided an affiliate id to compare the prices. ZeusCart supports you to provide the price runner’s affiliate id to make use of comparing the prices of products with other websites. Comparison Shopping Engine module helps you add the Price Runner affiliate id.

 

  1. Click Settings > CSE Settings will open Comparison Shopping Engine module.

 

 

  1. Pricerunner Affiliate ID: Enter the pricerunner id created using www.pricerunner.com.
  1. Click Save will display "Added Successfully" message.
