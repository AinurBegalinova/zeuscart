Products Sold (By Revenue)

Product Status business intelligence is to know about the status of products. In Products Sold (By Revenue), the graph will be shown based on the price of products that are sold. Using this, you can get an idea of all the products. So in this all the products that are sold will be displayed. The price of products that are selling in market will be taken. Admin can customize the product status based on the several criteria that for last 30 days, for current month, for last month, from the time when ZeusCart 2.3 installed and your own custom function based on the date.

 

  1. Click Business Intelligence > Product Status > Products Sold (By Revenue) will open Products Sold (By Revenue) page.
  • By default, the graph will be displayed for the price of products that are sold from the current date till last 30 days.
  1. Date Range: Using this list box, you can view the graph based on your own options by selecting the criteria listed. Select the option and click GO button will display the graph based on selected criteria.
  • Last 30 Days: The price of products that are sold for last 30 days (current date till last 30 days) will be displayed in a graph. In X - axis, the name of products that are sold in last 30 days will be taken and in Y - axis the price of products sold in last 30 days will be taken.
  • This Month: When you select this option then the price of products that are sold in this month will be displayed. In X - axis, the name of products that are sold in current month will be taken and in Y - axis the price of products sold in current month will be taken.
  • Last Month: The price of products that are sold in last month will be displayed. In X - axis, the name of products that are sold in last month will be taken and in Y - axis the price of products sold in last month will be taken.

 

 

  • All Time: This is simply based on the price of products that are sold from the time you have installed ZeusCart 2.3. In X - axis, the name of products that are sold from the time you have installed ZeusCart 2.3 will be taken and in Y - axis the price of products sold will be taken.
  • Custom: When you select this option, then the date fields will be displayed near by. Select the starting date and ending date between which you need to view about the price of products that are sold. In X - axis, the name of products that are sold between the dates you have selected will be taken and in Y - axis the price of products sold will be taken.
