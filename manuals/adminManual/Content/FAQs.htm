FAQs

ZeusCart 2.3 supports adding of Frequently Adding Questions. For ease use of Admin, we have just provided the two boxes to enter the Questions and Answers easily. Admin can make use of this module to convey any much important information to users in Questions and Answers format.

 

  1. Click CMS > FAQs will open Faqs page.
  2. The list of Questions and Answers currently added will be displayed in a grid with thw option to edit and delete it.

 

 

Creating new FAQs

  1. Click button will open Faqs page.

 

 

    • Question: Enter the question of FAQ to be displayed in site.
    • Answer: Enter the right answer or solution for the question you have entered.
    • Click Add FAQ button will display "FAQ Added Successfully!" message.
    • Click button will get you back to Faqs page.

 

Editing FAQs

  1. Click icon will open Faqs page.
    • Update the necessary information to be modified.
    • Click Update FAQ button will "Updated Successfully!" message.
    • Click button will get you back to Faqs page.

 

Deleting FAQs

  1. Click icon will display "Are you sure to delete?" message.
    • Click OK will display "Deleted Successfully" message.
    • Click Cancel will help you to exit from deletion.

We have provided Paging for viewing the FAQs list. Click on the page numbers to go to corresponding pages and click Prev and Next to go back and to go next to the page where you are now. By default Ten FAQs will be listed in a page.
