Export Product

ZeusCart 2.3 provides you separate module to export the Product details. Admin can export the Product details in four formats like Excel, XML, CSV and Tab file.

 

  • Click Catelog > Products > Export Product will open Export Product page.

 

 

  • Click on the respective format you need to export the report.
  • Click Export button, then you can download the file.

Suppose if you need to export the Product details in Excel format, and then select Excel from the list provided, then click "Export" button. It will open "Opening product_Details.xls" dialog box, Click "OK" will download excel file otherwise click "Cancel" to exit from downloading the file.
