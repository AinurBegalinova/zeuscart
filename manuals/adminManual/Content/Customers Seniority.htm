Customers Seniority

The customer Status business intelligence helps to understand the customer details easily. Using Customers Seniority, Admin can understand the customers joined based on weekly basis and monthly basis. The graph will be shown only based on the criteria that percentage of customers joined today, before one week, before one month, three months before, less than one year, and greater than one year. Admin can not change this order and can not customize the graph.

 

  1. Click Business Intelligence > Customer Status > Customers Seniority will open Customers Seniority page.
  2. The graph will be displayed for the percentage of customers joined with respect to customers joined today, before one week, before one month, three months before, less than one year, and greater than one year. Admin can not change this order and can not customize the graph.
