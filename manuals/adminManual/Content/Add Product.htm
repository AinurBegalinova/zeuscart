Add Product

Product entry helps Admin to add the product to be displayed in the site. It provides support to add the images for the product, specify the brand names, and much more. ZeusCart supports facility to add the Product details using tabs. This helps Admin to finish the product entry much easier. Each tab is separated for its special features like Category, General, Images, Attributes, Price, Inventory, Meta Information and Related Products

 

  1. Click Catalog > Products > View Product, will open Product List page.
  2. Click the button will open ADD PRODUCTS page to add the product information. By default, it opens the first tab i.e., Category tab.
    • Category

 

 

    • Select Main Category: Select the Category name displayed in list for which you need to add product.
    • Product Subcategory: Select the Sub Category name to be added from the list displayed.
    • Product Sub Under Sub category: Select the Sub Under Sub category name to be added from the list displayed.
    • Click Continue will direct you to next tab i.e., General tab.
  • General

 

 

      • Product Name: Enter the name of product to be displayed. It represents the title of product and which helps user in searching of product.
      • Product Description: Using WYSIWG editor, enter the complete description of product. The editor helps you to present an attractive description for users.
      • SKU: It is the Stock Keeping Unit in which Admin can enter unique code of the product to be displayed.
      • Brand: Admin should select the brand name of product listed from displaying list box. If your brand is not available in the list, then click Others link, enter the name of your brand in the displaying text box.
      • Model: Enter the model name of your product to be added.
      • Produc Tags: The keywords that are related to product name, which is to be used while searching the product should be entered. When user enters the keyword related to the added product tags then your product will be displayed for the user. Enter the tags separated by commas.

Suppose if your product name is Apple IPod Shuffle, then you can enter apple, iPod, shuffle, iPod shuffle and etcâ€¦ in the product tags field with comma separated. This helps user when he search for a product, he can just enter the keyword like apple or iPod in the Keyword text box, it will list all products which has apple or iPod keywords added in the Product Tags. Remember No matter in what category the keyword related product has been listed

      • Introduction Date: Select the date in which you need to schedule the product to be displayed in site. The date can be selected from scheduled calendar displayed.
      • Is CSE Enabled: If you have registered with the www.pricerunner.com, you may get the pricerunner id to compare the prices of products added in your website with the other websites. When you enable the check box, ZeusCart 2.3 will ask you enter the CSE Keyword, then your product will be compared with the other products based on the entered keyword. You can enter the keywords related to product.

      Suppose if you are entering details for Sony Camera product, then you can enter the keywords like sony, camera, digital camera, etc.

      • Is Featured: Enable the check box to display the product in featured products list provided in home page of site.
      • Visibility at Store Front: Enable the check box to make activate of product in site.
      • Click Continue will get you to Images tab and Click Back to go back to the Category tab again.
    • Images

 

 

      • Main Product Image: Click Browse to upload the image for your product.
      • Add Multiple Images: Click on this link will help you to add the multiple images for a product. When you click this will display Sub Product Image field. Click Browse button to add the images. Click Remove link to delete or cancel the addition of multiple images.
      • Click Continue will get you to Attributes tab and Click Back to go back to the General tab again.

The added image of any size will be shrunken in to the size of image that you have added while installing the package.

    • Attributes

 

 

      • The list of attributes will be displayed based on the attributes added while creating the Sub Category details.
      • Select the attributes to be added using the available Attribute types listed.
      • Click Continue will get you to Price tab and Click Back to go back to the Attributes tab again.
    • Price

 

 

      • Price: Enter the price that you have allotted for your product in the site. Admin can fix the price to be allotted for a product. The entered price will be considered in $.
      • Product Msrp: Enter the actual price of product allotted by the manufacturer of product.
      • Tier Price: Click Add button will display the respective fields for adding Tier Price.
        • Quantity: Enter the number of quantity that you are going to allot for product in the text box provided.
        • Msrp Per Unit: Enter the amount to be collected for each product.
        • Click Remove link to delete or cancel the addition of Tier Price.
      • Product Shipping Cost USD: Enter the amount to be collected while sending the product to customers.
      • Click Continue will direct you to next tab i.e., Inventory tab and Click Back to get you back to Attributes page.
    • Inventory

 

 

      • Re-Order Level Quantity: Enter the value to be taken for Re-Order level. When product reaches the value entered in Re-Order level after purchase of users, then it will be mentioned as Out of Stock in site.
      • Stock on Hand Quantity: Enter the number of products available with you currently.
      • Click Continue will direct you to next tab i.e., Meta Information tab and Click Back to get you back to Inventory page.

If you have entered the quantity of product available in hand is 10 and entered the re-order level as 3, then when the products are purchased by several users and reached the re-order level of 3 then the product will be displayed as Out of Stock in site.

    • Meta Information

 

 

      • Meta Keywords: This entry is essential as it provides meta-information about your page, such as descriptions and keywords for search engines and refreshes rates. Create effective well formatted Meta tags for your website with this Meta tag generator. Enter the essential keywords to be used for product.
      • Meta Description: Enter the brand name for product i.e., the name allotted by manufacturer for product.
      • Click Continue will get you to Related Products tab and Click Back to go back to the Meta Information tab again.
    • Related Products

 

 

      • The list of all products currently added will be displayed in a grid with the Product Name, Brand, MSRP and Price.
      • Search: Helps you to search the products easily and selecting the related products.
      • Enter the keyword that you know about the product in the respective text boxes provided.
      • Click Search will display the list of products based on the entered keyword.
      • Click on the Check Box to select the related products to be listed.
      • Click Reset Filter button to clear the search and list all the products.
      • Click Finish button will display "Product <name of the added product> Inserted Successfully" message in PRODUCT LIST page.
      • Click Back to go back to the Meta Information page.
