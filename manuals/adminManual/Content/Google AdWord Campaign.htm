Google AdWord Campaign

AdWords is Google's flagship advertising product and it offers pay-per-click (PPC) advertising, and site-targeted advertising for both text and banner ads. The AdWords program includes local, national, and international distribution. Google's text advertisements are short, consisting of one title line and two content text lines. Image ads can be one of several different Interactive Advertising Bureau (IAB) standard sizes.

ZeusCart 2.3 helps you in advertising your products in Google website. To give advertisement in Google you should get register with http://www.google.com/adwords. Then in a single click you can download all the product details in the needed format for Google. You can upload that file in Google then your products will be advertised in Google website and you will be paid some amount for every click on your products by Google users.

 

  1. Click Advertisement > Google AdWord Campaign will open Google AdWord page.

 

 

  1. Click on Click Here link will open "adword.tsv" dialog box.
    • Click OK to download the Excel file with all product information.
    • Click Cancel to exit from downloading the file.
  1. Save the Excel file in your system and open http://www.google.com/adwords website.
    • Login with your Username and Password registered with Google Base.
    • And follow the instructions provided in Google Adwords to upload the excel file.
