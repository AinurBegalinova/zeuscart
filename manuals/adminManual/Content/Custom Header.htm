Custom Header

ZeusCart allocates separate place to display the content for header. Admin can make information to be displayed in header like announcing the offer, latest news and much more. He can provide attractive header content getting displayed with the help of WYSIWYG editor. The added header content will be displayed in top of the home page.

 

  1. Click Settings > Site Settings > Custom Header will open Custom Header page.

 

 

  1. Enter the content to get displayed in the place allocated for content header in home page. Make attractive header using WYSIWYG editor.
  2. Click Update Custom Header will display "Custom Header changed Successfully" message.
