Site Logo

The designing of logo promotes business professionally in the market and it helps in attracting potential customers and client to business. Logo design gives you a unique and innovative identity to the business. ZeusCart provides you facility to add logo for your site and which helps in giving the first and best impression for your business.

 

  1. Click Settings > Site Settings > Site Logo will open Site Logo page.

 

 

  1. Click Browse button to upload the image of logo to be added. The path of added logo will be displayed in text box provided. By default, the added logo image of any size will be reduced in to 239x76 dimensions.
  2. Click Upload Logo will display "Updated Successfully" message.
