View Customers

ZeusCart 2.3 provides user-friendly customer management. It helps you to maintain site user’s information in a separate module. Admin can view the entire information of all customers added in site. Admin has the privilege to activate or inactivate the customers, whenever needed. Admin can update the information of customers and can delete the customer details.

 

  1. Click Customers > View Customers will open Show Customer Details page.
    • The list of all customers added in site will be displayed with the information of Display Name, First Name, Last Name, Email, Options to Edit/Delete/Change Status.

 

 

  1. Search: We provide you search functionality to search your needed customers easily.
    • Enter the keyword of customer details that you know in the provided text boxes under the respective field names.
    • Click Search button will display the list of customers based on the given information.
  1. Click icon, will display "Are You sure to delete this record" message.
    • Click OK will remove the selected customer from site.
    • Click Cancel will help you to exit from deletion.

 

ZeusCart provides you support for activating customer once they have registered with the site. When a Customer is registered his profile in the site, then his information will be listed to activate the customer for his further process and also Admin has the authority to deactivate the customer whenever needed.

    • To activate a customer, Click icon provided corresponding to the customer, then it will display "Successfully Activated" message.
    • To deactivate a customer, Click icon provided it will display "Successfully Inactivated" message.

We have provided Paging for viewing the customer details. Click on the page numbers to go to corresponding pages and click Prev and Next to go back and to go next to the page where you are now. By default Ten customers will be listed in a page.
