Customers (By Location)

The customer Status business intelligence helps to understand the customer details easily. In Customers (By Location) module, Admin can understand the number of customers joined in ZeusCart 2.3 based on location. The list of new customers will be displayed in a graph for better understanding of Admin. Admin can get the idea about number of customers that has been joined. It will be displayed based on the customers that are joined with respect to the location. So it is totally only the customers that are joined successfully by location. For ease use of Admin, we have provided number of functionalities to know about customers. Admin can customize the customer details based on the several criteria that for last 30 days, for current month, for last month, from the time when ZeusCart 2.3 installed and your own custom function based on the date.

 

  1. Click Business Intelligence > Customer Status > Customers (By Location) will open Customers (By Location) page.
  • By default, the graph will be displayed for the name of countries in which customers are joined from the current date till last 30 days.
  1. Date Range: Using this list box, you can view the graph based on your own options by selecting the criteria listed. Select the option and click GO button will display the graph based on selected criteria.
  • Last 30 Days: The name of countries in which customers are joined for last 30 days (current date till last 30 days) will be displayed in a graph. In X - axis, the name of countries in which customers joined in last 30 days will be taken and in Y - axis the quantity of customers will be taken.

 

 

  • This Month: When you select this option then the customers that are joined in this month will be displayed. In X - axis, the name of countries in which customers joined in current month will be taken and in Y - axis the quantity of customers will be taken.
  • Last Month: The customers that are joined in last month will be displayed. In X - axis, the name of countries in which customers joined in last month will be taken and in Y - axis the quantity of customers will be taken.
  • All Time: This is simply based on the customers that are joined from the time you have installed ZeusCart 2.3. In X - axis, the name of countries in which customers joined from the time you have installed ZeusCart 2.3 till the name of current year and in Y - axis the quantity of customers will be taken.
  • Custom: When you select this option, then the date fields will be displayed near by. Select the starting date and ending date between which you need to view about the customers that are joined. In X - axis, the name of countries in which customers joined will be taken and in Y - axis the quantity of orders will be taken.
