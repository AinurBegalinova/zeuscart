About Us

Using About Us module, Admin can enter the details of your company. You can enter your company history using this module. Admin can make an outstanding content using WYSIWYG editor. It helps Admin to enter the content simple and valuable.

 

  1. Click CMS > About Us will open About Us page.

 

 

  1. Enter the content to be displayed, when user clicks About Us link provided in footer of site.
  2. Click Update About Us button will display "About Us Content Changed Successfully" message.
