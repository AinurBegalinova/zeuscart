News Management

In ZeusCart 2.3, we have added a new module for news management. Using News Management, Admin can provide latest news regarding site or can use this news management to announce any latest news and much more. Admin alone can add the content to be displayed in News. News can be viewed by any one who visits the site and the user do not wants to be a member for viewing the News added. Admin can add any number of news contents to be listed in site. Admin has privilege to change the status of news to be listed from active to inactive and vice versa. Admin can modify/delete the news contents, whenever necessary.

 

  1. Click Promotions > News Management will open News List page.
  • The list of news that are currently created will be displayed in a grid with the information of News Title, Created Date and the Status.
  1. Click button will open NEWS page.

 

 

  • News Name: Enter the name of news to be displayed. The entered text here will be displayed as heading for the news content.
  • News Content: Enter the content of news to be displayed based on the title added. Using text editor, you can create the contents easily and make them attractive. The content should be minimum of 10 characters.
  • Click Add News will add the news in the list of news but which will be inactivated.
  • Click button will get you back to News List page.
  1. Changing the Status

 

 

  • To activate the news, Click icon provided corresponding to the news, then it will display "Successfully Activated" message.
  • To deactivate the news, Click icon provided it will display "Successfully Inactivated" message.
  1. Deleting the news
  • Select the news which needs to be deleted using the checkbox available corresponding to each news.
  • Click button will display "News Deleted Successfully" message.
