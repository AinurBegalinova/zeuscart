Products Bulk Upload

Using Bulk Upload module, Admin can upload unlimited number of products in a single click. Admin can upload the products in TSV file (Tab Separated Value) format. Admin can download the sample file from ZeusCart 2.3 in Zip format. You can download two files named Product and Category ids. In categoryids file, you can view the list of category names and its corresponding id values. product file is your main file where you should enter the product details. In the first line it consists of the fields names to enter the product details. you should not make any corrections/modifications in the field names. Next to the field names you can start entering your product details one by one. After finishing all your entries save the file and you just upload it in ZeusCart 2.3 then automatically all the products will be added in ZeusCart 2.3. This Bulk Upload saves your time in adding product details step by step and do not need any internet facility while adding the product details.

 

  1. Click Catalog > Products > Products Bulk Upload will open Products Bulk Upload page.
  2. Sample Product TSV File: Click icon will open a dialog box to download the sample Zip file.

 

 

    • After downloading Sample file from ZeusCart 2.3, you will be seeing the two files in Zip folder named Product and categoryids.
    • In categoryids file you can view the list of category names with the corresponding ids generated.
    • In product file you can view the field names such as category_id, title description, Stock Keeping Unit, brand model, Manufacturer suggested Retail Price , price to be allotted by Admin, weight, dimension, shipping_cost, status, tag, meta_description, meta_keywords, is_featured, Re-Order Level, Stock On Hand.

You should not make any corrections in the field names and you can enter the product details based on field names provided. You can see the details of field names in Add Product chapter.

    • After completing the product entry save the product file.
  1. Product TSV File: Click Browse button to upload the TSV file in which you have completed your all product entries.
  2. Click Submit will display "Product(s) Created Successfully" message.
