Google Base Data Feed

Google Base is an online database provided by Google into which any user can add almost any type of content, such as text, images, and structured information in formats like XML, PDF, Excel, RTF, Word Perfect. If Google finds it relevant it may appear on its shopping search engine, Google Maps or even the web search. The piece of content can then be labeled with attributes like the ingredients for a recipe or the camera model for stock photography. Google subsequently responded on their blog with an official statement.

Using this module, Admin can feed all products in the Google website. The feature of this data feed is like providing advertisements to all products. But the transactions on the product will be diverted to Admin itself. To feed products in the Google website, Google follows the standard format of posting the products. And that format is downloaded and linked in ZeusCart 2.3. So just by downloading the format in a file and uploading it, will feed all the products information in the downloaded file. Then opening the Google website and uploading the same will add all products in to their website.

 

  1. Click Advertisement > Google Base will open Google Base page.

 

 

  1. Click on Click Here link will open "GoogleBase_Product_Feed.xls" dialog box.
    • Click OK to download the Excel file with all product information.
    • Click Cancel to exit from downloading the file.
  1. Save the Excel file in your system and open http://www.google.com/base website.
    • Login with your Username and Password registered with Google Base.
    • And follow the instructions provided in Google Base to upload the excel file.
