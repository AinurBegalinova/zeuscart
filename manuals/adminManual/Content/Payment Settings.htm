Payment Settings

Using this setting, Admin can set online payment information needed while marketing the product. By default, this setting comes with Payment gateways integration like PayPal, Google CheckOut, Authorize.net, 2checkout, worldpay, Paymate Express, Money Bookers, PSi Gate, StormPay, AlertPay, Your Pay, iTransact, BluePay and SafePay. Admin should give his payment gateway’s account id, thereby while doing transactions considered easy to check out the payments in correct account number. Admin can select the payment gateways by enabling the checkbox available. If you need to add any new payment gateway, please contact our Tech Support for your help in AJ Square Inc at http://support.ajsquare.com/.

 

  1. Click Settings > Payment Settings will open Payment Gateways Management page.

 

 

  • Payment Name: The name of payment gateway will be displayed.
  • Status: If you wish to select any payment gateways, then enable the checkbox available near the payment gateways.
  • Details: Enter the details of payment gateways for which you have selected.
  1. Click Update will add the information of payment gateway.
