Most Searched Keywords

ZeusCart 2.2 provides you an advanced feature of displaying the keywords which has been searched more frequently. All the entered keyword will be displayed in Admin Console. Admin can view all the keywords that are searched frequently in the order of which has been searched high. It helps Admin to know which is the fast moving product in market.

 

  1. Click Others > Most Searched Keywords will open MOST SEARCHED KEYWORDS page.
  2. The list of most frequently searched keywords will be displayed in the order of high to low priority in a grid. It displays the information of Searched Keyword and Search Frequency.

 

 

We have provided Paging for viewing the searched keywords. Click on the page numbers to go to corresponding pages and click Prev and Next to go back and to go next to the page where you are now. By default Ten keywords will be listed in a page.
