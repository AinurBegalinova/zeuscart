Processing Orders

This business intelligence is also fully based on the status of orders made by Admin. When user ordered a product, then it will listed in Order List page. Then Admin has the authority to change the status. When Admin changes the order status in processing then it will be taken in to consideration to show in processing orders business intelligence. So it is completely based on the status of orders that are processing. In this, Admin can customize the orders based on the several criteria that for last 30 days, for current month, for last month, from the time when ZeusCart 2.3 installed and your own custom function based on the date.

 

  1. Click Business Intelligence > Order Status > Processing Orders will open Orders under Processing page.
  • By default, the graph will be displayed for the orders that are processing from the current date till last 30 days.
  1. Date Range: Using this list box, you can view the graph based on your own options by selecting the criteria listed. Select the option and click GO button will display the graph based on selected criteria.
  • Last 30 Days: The orders that are processing for last 30 days (current date till last 30 days) will be displayed in a graph. In X - axis, the dates of last 30 days will be taken and in Y - axis the quantity of orders will be taken.
  • This Month: When you select this option then the orders that are processing in this month will be displayed. In X - axis, the name of current month will be taken and in Y - axis the quantity of orders will be taken.
  • Last Month: The orders that are processing in last month will be displayed. In X - axis, the name of last month will be taken and in Y - axis the quantity of orders will be taken.

 

 

  • All Time: This is simply based on the orders that are processing from the time you have installed ZeusCart 2.3. In X - axis, the list of year names in which you have installed ZeusCart 2.3 till the name of current year and in Y - axis the quantity of orders will be taken.
  • Custom: When you select this option, then the date fields will be displayed near by. Select the starting date and ending date between which you need to view about the processing orders details In X - axis, the dates you have selected will be taken and in Y - axis the quantity of orders will be taken..
