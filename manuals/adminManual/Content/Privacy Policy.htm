Privacy Policy

Using Privacy Policy module, Admin can enter the detailed description of the policies to be used when using the website. Admin can make an outstanding content using WYSIWYG editor. It helps Admin to enter the content simple and valuable.

 

  1. Click CMS > Privacy Policy will open Privacy Policy page.

 

 

  1. Enter the content to be displayed, when user clicks Privacy Policy link provided in footer of site.
  2. Click Update Privacy Policy button will display "Privacy Policy Updated Successfully" message.
