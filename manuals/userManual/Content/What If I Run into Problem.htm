What If I Run into Problem

That’s what we’re here for. Setting up ZeusCart 2.3 and getting it configured just the way you want it can be confusing the first time through. It gets easier each time, however, so don’t worry if you’re confronted with a problem.

Simply call our support through http://support.ajsquare.com/ and one of our knowledgeable consultants will help you through the set up and configuration process.

It really isn’t too difficult and once you have started the set-up and configuration process, the on-screen directions are extremely helpful – screen driven as they say.

ZeusCart 2.3 is a full service, endlessly adaptable checkout system that lets you take orders from countries around the world (with automatic currency exchange), add and delete products from your catalog, change the look of the checkout, change the information required to make a purchase, manage inventory and order fulfillment – it’s all there and more.

So give it a try and if you do run into a problem, give us a call. We’re here to help you when you need it. After all, we recognize that our success depends on your success and we want you to succeed.
