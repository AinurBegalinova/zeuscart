Popular Tags

This is a new feature added in ZeusCart 2.3. When all the users searches for a product using some Keywords. The keywords which has been used for searching more frequently will be listed in Popular Tags section. The most frequently searched keyword will be displayed in Large font size and the lowest searched keyword will be displayed in Small font. Clicking on keyword you can view the list of products available based on that keyword. By default, 50 tags will be displayed in this section.

 

  1. Click on any keyword that is listed in Popular Tags section which is available in Home Page.

You can view Popular Tags in Sub Category, Products and My Cart pages also.

  1. The list of products available based on the selected keyword will be displayed.

 

 

    • Click on the name of product to view Product Details page.
    • Add the particular product to Cart, Wishlist and Compare.
    • From this page, you can perform Narrow Search also to customize your search for finding perfect product.
