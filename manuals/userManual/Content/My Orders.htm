My Orders

ZeusCart 2.3 provides adverse support in tracking your order details. When user has completed the checkout process then the product details will be added in My Orders page. You can view the order information like Order number, Date in which you have the product, Total Price of Order, Status of Order like whether it is pending or processing or delivered, and Track Id with which you can know the status of product from the Shipping Company. You can view the complete order details like shipping address and Billing Address that you have given while checkout and so on.

 

  1. Login to your account using Email Address and Password. Then you will be getting in to Account Dashboard page.
  2. Click "My Orders" link available in the left column on My Account section will open "My Orders" page.
    • You can view the order details displayed in a grid with the information of Order number, Order date, Date in which you have the product, Total Price of Order, Status of Order like whether it is pending or processing or delivered, and Track Id with which you can know the status of product from the Shipping Company.

 

 

Track Id will be displayed only when the status is processing or delivered. Using Track Id available you can know the product status from shipping company just by clicking it.

  1. Click on Order Number you will be directed to Order Details page.
    • You can view the order status, recipient name, total price, order date, close date, payment details like payment id and payment gateway name, billing address and shipping address that you have given when checkout, product details like product name, price, quantity, shipping charge and total amount.
