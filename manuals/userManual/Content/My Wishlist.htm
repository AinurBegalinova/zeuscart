My Wishlist

Using this module, user can view all the products that are added to wishlist. User can use this facility to monitor closely on the favorite products. From the wishlist page, user can view entire product information and has privilege to add product to the shopping cart. User can browse for the product and can add favorite products to the wishlist. User can remove the products that are added in the wishlist, whenever necessary. In ZeusCart 2.3, we have provided a new feature of sending your favorite products to your Friends.

 

  1. Login to your account using Email Address and Password. Then you will be getting in to Account Dashboard page.
  2. Click "My Wishlist" link available in the left column on My Account section will open "My Wishlist" page.
    • The list of products added in Wishlist will be displayed a grid with the information like Product image, Name of Product, the date in which it is added to wishlist and options to add product to cart and remove the product.

 

 

    • Click on the Product Image or Name link will direct you to "Product Detail Page" to view the entire description of product.
    • Add to Cart: Click on this link will add the product to cart and opens "Your Shopping Cart" page to view the list of products added in your shopping cart and from there you can Checkout the product.
    • Click icon provided will delete the product from your wishlist page.
    • Send to friend
      • Enter E-Mail Id: Enter the email address of your friend to whom you wish to send your favorite products.
      • Click button will send all the products that are available in your wishlist and will display "Mail has been sent!" message.
