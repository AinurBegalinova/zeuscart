Getting Inside of ZeusCart 2.3 | Registration Process

 

Registration Process

To become a member of ZeusCart 2.3, you must first register. Registering on ZeusCart 2.3 is easy and free. The Registration page will ask you to enter display name, user name email id, and password. You must reenter the Password for verification. You will also be asked to enable the option for subscribing newsletter and to accept the terms & conditions of site. Only if you enable the option for terms & conditions, you will be permitted to register with the site. Once you have submitted your registration, you will receive a confirmation email message from ZeusCart 2.3 and you will have instant access to ZeusCart 2.3 services.

The registration process includes just a few simple steps:

 

  1. Click Start here link available in Header of Home page, then it will direct you to User Information page where you can do registration.

 

 

    • Display Name: Enter the name to be displayed after you login to the site. Using this name you will known to all users and Admin.
    • First Name: Enter the first name of your name.
    • Last Name: Enter the last name of your name. You can enter the initial too.

If your name is John Prakash you can enter John in First Name box and Prakash in Last Name box. Suppose if your name is Savian. R then you can enter Savian in First Name box and R in Last Name box.

    • Email Address: Enter the email id which you will use frequently. Because all your transactions and all your activities will be sent to the entered mail id. It helps to get your password when you forgot it.
    • Password: You can give the password of your choice and which must be minimum of 6 characters and maximum of any characters. Enter the password that you can easily remember but cannot guess by others.
    • Confirm Password: Once again enter the password that you have given in Password field for verification.

You will use your email id and password every time you login to ZeusCart 2.3.

  • Address: Enter your address details. You can enter your residential address or official address.
  • City: Enter the name of your city.
  • State/Province: Enter the name of your state.
  • Zip/Postal Code: Enter the corresponding postal code of your country and state entered.
  • Country: Enter the name of your country.
  • Enter Code: As displaying in the figure you should enter the code in the text box. You can change the code by clicking "Try another one" link.
  • Subscribe Newsletter: Enable the check box, if you like to subscribe for the Newsletter to be sent by Admin. The newsletter will be sent to your mail id you have entered.
    • Accept Terms & Condition: Click on "Accept Terms & Condition" link to view the terms and conditions used in ZeusCart. Then enable the check box only if you are satisfied with the terms and conditions given.
  1. Click "Continue" will display "Account has been Created Successfully" message.

 

Check your email

You will check your email for a registration confirmation mail from ZeusCart 2.3 and follow the instructions in email to confirm your registration and complete registration process.
