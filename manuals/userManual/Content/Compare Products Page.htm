Compare Products Page

Compare page helps you to compare the features of selected products. The products will be compared based on the products available in the same sub category. User can add number of products to compare it and can remove the product from comparison whenever necessary. You can add the products to Compare section from Product Details Page and also from the Page where all the products are listed based on search.

 

  1. Find the product using Search or by browsing.
  2. Select the Products that are to be compared from same Sub Category.

Suppose if you have products like Dresses for Men and Dresses for Women in the Sub Category Dresses. Then you can select Dresses for Men and Dresses for Women products to compare its features. Suppose if you have Product Dresses for Men in Men Dresses Sub Category and Dresses for Women in Women Dresses Sub Category then you can not compare Dresses for Men and Dresses for Women products.

  1. The added product names will be displayed in the "Compare Products" section available in Right Column of the home page.
  2. Click button will direct you to the "Compare Products" page to view the products listed with their comparison.
    • You can view the list of products selected for comparison with all its features displayed in a grid.

 

 

    • If you wish to remove a product from comparison click "Remove" link provided.
    • Click button will get you to "Your Shopping Cart" page.

 

Remember that User can add the products to compare which are available only in same Sub Category.
