Using Keyword Search

If you know only the keyword for product, then you can use keyword search to list the products available based on your entered keywords.

 

  1. Enter the Keyword in the text box available in header of the home page.
  2. Select the category that you need from the nearby list box.
  3. Click GO button, will list the products that are related to the selected category and entered keywords.

 

 

    • Click on the name of product to view Product Details page.
    • Add the particular product to Cart, Wishlist and Compare.
    • From this page, you can perform Narrow Search also to customize your search for finding perfect product.
