Product Detail Page

When a user clicks on the image or name of product, it will direct him to detail page to view the entire information about product. From the detail page, user can review on product and can add product to shopping cart. User has the facility to add product to his wishlist and to compare with the other products. ZeusCart 2.3 provides special feature to bookmark the detail page in your browser to view it whenever necessary. Product Detail Page displays the detailed description of product and features of product, Quick information, and additional information like availability, Shipping Cost, Tags and much more. This page list the similar products links added for selected product. Click on the links you can view the description for similar products.

 

  1. Find the product using Search or by browsing.
  2. Click on the image or name of product for which you need to view the complete description.

 

 

The Main Page of the Product Detail page displays,

  • Bread crumbs: Displays the path in which product is added. The bread crumb displays the category name and subcategory name in which the product is added.
  • Intro of Product: The image and name of selected product will be displayed. The price will be displayed based on the tier price added. The ending and starting price of the product will be displayed. The added reviews will be listed with their rating stars. The entered tags and shipping cost of corresponding product will be displayed. It shows the information about stock of product.

If the product has reached the re order level entered then the Availability of product will be displayed as "No"

    • Compare Price: If Admin has enabled price runner id for the product then button will be displayed. Click on this button will open "Compare Price" page.
      1. You can view the selected product available in different websites with the retailer names, available stock, prices and link to connect to that store.

 

 

    • Add to Cart: Click on this button will add the product to cart and opens "Your Shopping Cart" page to view the list of products added in your shopping cart.
    • Add to Wishlist: Click on this link will add the viewing product in to your wishlist and opens "My Wish List" page and displays "Product added successfully into your wishlist" message.
    • Add to Compare: When user click on this link, the product will be added to Compare Products section available in left column.
    • Bookmark: ZeusCart 2.3’s Product Detail Page supports you to bookmark the products page in your browser. This helps user to view the favorite product whenever necessary.
      1. Click button will open "Add Bookmark" dialog box.
      2. Name: Enter any name or title for your bookmarking product.
      3. Create in: Select the Bookmark Folder in which you wish to add your product.
      4. Click "Add" button will add the product in your selected bookmark folder.
      5. Click "Cancel" to skip from doing bookmark of the product page.
  • Tabs
    • Description: The full description of product will be displayed. User can read the entire description and can get the full idea about product.
    • Features: In this section, the extra details of product like Model, SKU, dimensions, weight and attributes added will be displayed. This helps user to know the complete detail about product.
    • Tier Price: ZeusCart 2.3 provides support for Admin to add the tier price of product. The added tier price will be displayed in this Section. User can view the tier price allotted for product. Using which user can order for product based on the needed number of quantity of product.
  • Who Bought This Item Also Bought
    • The list of products which are bought while purchasing the particular product will be displayed. The products listed with their image, name and price.
    • Click on the Name or Image of that product you can view the details of that product.
  • Write Your Own Review
      1. Rate this Product: Select the respective Stars to rate the product.
      2. Caption: Enter the short summary of product that you wish to review.
      3. Review: Enter the detailed description of your review.
      4. Enter Code: As displaying in the figure you should enter the code in the text box. You can change the code by clicking "Try another one" link.
      5. Click button will display "Your review has been accepted for moderation" message.

 

The Left Column of Product Detail Page consists of

  • Browse Similar Items: The related products added by Admin will be displayed in this section. It helps user to know the related products detail and Admin may list the accessories for the selected product. User can get complete information about the product and also its accessories using this single page. Click on the name of product link to view details page of that product.
  • Select Currency: Click on the country name you can view the product's price will be displayed based on the selected country name. You can use this currency only to view the price of products and in the process of checkout. But when it leads to payment gateways to make the payment, you can checkout only with primary currency selected by Admin.

 

 

  • Compare Products: When user clicks "ADD To COMPARE" button from product detail page, the product will be added here.
    • Click button will direct you to the "Compare Products" page to view the products listed with their comparison.
    • To delete all the products added to compare, click button will display "You have no items to compare" message.
  • Recently Viewed: The list of products that are recently viewed by user will be displayed and from there user can view the product details easily. The products will be displayed with their images, name, ratings given and price. The products will be listed based on the First In First Out format. By default, four products will be displayed.
    • Click on the name or image of the product to view the complete details of product.
    • Click Quick Info button to view the details of product.
  • Popular Tags: The keywords that are searched most frequently will be displayed. The most frequently searched keyword will be displayed in Large font size and the lowest searched keyword will be displayed in Small font. Clicking on keyword you can view the list of products available based on that keyword. By default, 50 tags will be displayed.
