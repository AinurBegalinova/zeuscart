Quick Checkout as Guest User

Using ZeusCart 2.3, user can checkout the products in two different ways like without registration and after registration. When a user not yet registered with the site but needs to checkout a product, then can checkout using Quick checkout method. If a guest user needs to checkout a product then he can give his email address and can proceed with the checkout process.

 

  1. Find the product using Search or by browsing.
  2. Click on the image or name of product will get you to Product Detail Page to know the complete information of that product.
  3. Click on button will add the product to cart and opens "Your Shopping Cart" page to view the list of products added in shopping cart.
  4. Click checkout button will get you to Account Details page.
  5. In Quick Registration section,

 

 

    • E-mail Address: Enter the email id which you will use frequently. Because all your transactions and all your activities will be sent to the entered mail id.
    • Password: You can give the password of your choice and which must be minimum of 6 characters and maximum of any characters. Enter the password that you can easily remember but cannot guess by others.
    • Enter Code: As displaying in the figure you should enter the code in the text box. You can change the code by clicking "Try another one" link. 
  • Click Continue button will direct you to "Your Shopping Cart" page to view the list of products added in shopping cart.
  • Click checkout button will get you to Address Details page.
  1. In Address Details page,

 

 

    • Billing Address
      • Use Address From Address Book: Click on this option will display the list of contacts you have added in your Address Book. Select the contact you wish to add. Since you are a Guest User, No address will be displayed.
      • Use a New Address
        1. Name: Enter the name in which the product should be delivered.
        2. Company: Enter your company name if needed since it is an optional field.
        3. Address: Enter your billing address to which the product should be sent.
        4. City: Enter the name of your city.
        5. SubUrb: Enter the name of your sub urban if it is applicable. Because it is an optional field.
        6. State/Province: Enter the name of your state.
        7. Country: Select your country name from the list box available.
        8. Zip/Postal Code: Enter the Zip/Postal code of your city.
        9. Use Billing Address as Shipping Address: Click on this check box if shipping address is same as you have entered for your billing address else do not check the check box.
        10. Save Address To Address Book: When you enable this checkbox the entered new address will be saved in your Address Book available in your My Account page.
    • Shipping Address: If your shipping address is not same as billing address then click
      • Use Address From Address Book: Click on this option will display the list of contacts you have added in your Address Book. Select the contact you wish to add. Since you are a Guest User, No address will be displayed.
      • Use a New Address: Use the methods as mentioned in steps i to viii.
    • Click Continue button will direct you to Order Confirmation page.
  1. In Order Confirmation page,
    • The products will be displayed in a grid with the information of Product Name and Image, Unit Price, Quantity and Sub Total. Then the details of product will be displayed with total amount of products, shipping amount for all products, tax applied for products and the grand total of products available.
    • Coupon Code: If you are applying Coupon Code for the product then you can add the Coupon Code sent by Admin in the available text box and click Go button. If you apply coupon code for product then the Coupon discount facility like Discount Percentage or Discount on Price will be redeemed only from Actual Price not the tier price of product.

If your checking out product has Tier Price value, then you cannot use the Coupon facility. Suppose if you are not going to use coupon facility to buy that product, then you can buy that product with its tier price.

    • Click Proceed To Payment button will direct you to Payment Details page.
  1. In Payment Details page,
    • The list of Payment Gateways will be displayed, click on the Payment Gateway image through which you wish to make payment for your product.
