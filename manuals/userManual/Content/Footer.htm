Footer

The footer is the bottom section of home page. This section is where you usually put your copyright notice, link to your privacy policy and your website contact information and much more. In ZeusCart 2.3 footer, we have added a new feature of displaying the Hit Counter. It displays the count for which the users has visited the site. The number of times a user visiting the site the count will be updated in hit counter.
