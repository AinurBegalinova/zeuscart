ZeusCart 2.3 Home Page | Left Navigation

 

Left Navigation

The Left Navigation of Home Page is used for browsing the products based on categories, subscribing for newsletter and displays the products that are recently viewed by users. The recently viewed products will be displayed only after the current user has viewed any products.

 

 

  • Browse Categories: The list of categories added in site will be displayed. Click on the category link which you need to browse a product and follow the links to view your favorite product.
  • Newsletter: It helps user to subscribe for Newsletter to be sent even they do not register with the site. Enter the email id to which the newsletter to be sent by Admin in the provided text box. Click "Go" button will display "Newsletter subscription added for your account" message.
  • Recently Viewed: The list of products viewed by user will be displayed and from there user can view the product details easily. The products will be displayed with their images, name, ratings given and price. The products will be listed based on the First In First Out format. By default, four products will be displayed.
    • Click on the name or image of the product to view the complete details of product.
    • Click Quick Info button will open a "Quick Information" page where you can view a information of Products.

 

 

    • From this Page,
      1. Click on button will open Comparison Price page where the selected product will be compared with the other sites.
      2. Add to Cart: Click on this button will add the product to cart and opens "Your Shopping Cart" page to view the list of products added in your shopping cart.
      3. Add to Wishlist: Click on this link will add the viewing product in to your wishlist and opens "My Wish List" page and displays "Product added successfully into your wishlist" message.
      4. Click button will get you to Product Detail Page to view the complete information of Product.
      5. Click Close link to exit from Quick Info window.
  • Latest News: The news that are added recently by Admin will be displayed here. The news can be added only by Admin. By default, the two news will be displayed. Using "More..." link you can view all the news that are added by Admin.
