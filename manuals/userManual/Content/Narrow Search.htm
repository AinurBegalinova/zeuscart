Narrow Search

ZeusCart 2.3 provides you support to find the product easy using the narrow search. This search helps you in advanced search of product by specifying the brand names and price range. It helps you to increase your interest in searching and it gives you the perfect product that you are searching for.

You can do Narrow Search from two different pages like Home Page and Product Listing Page. Basically you can understand that Narrow Search will be displayed in the page where all the searched products are listed.

 

  1. Click "GO" button available in the top of home page with search panel or just select the category in which you need to search.
  2. You can view the list of products available,
    1. If you have selected the category then the products added in the category will be displayed with their thumbnail images.
    2. If you just click the GO button then all the products available in site will be displayed with their thumbnail images.
  3. The Narrow Search displays
    1. Price Range: Enter the range of product price between the products are added, then click "Go" button will display list of products available in the entered range.
    2. Brand: The brand names added for all products will be listed. Click the brand you wish to select, then the list of products available in the selected category will be displayed. The number of products available in the specified brand will be displayed for ease use.
    3. From this Page,

 

 

      • Click on the name of product to view Product Details page.
      • Add the particular product to Cart, Wishlist and Compare.
