Account Information

Account Information page helps user to update the personal information that they have entered while registration. User has privilege to change the email id, first name, last name and to change the current password.

 

  1. Login to your account using Email Address and Password. Then you will be getting in to Account Dashboard page.
  2. Click "Account Information" link available in the left column on My Account section will open "Edit Account Information" page.

 

 

  1. Account Information
    • Displays the information of First Name, Last Name and Email Address given while registration.
    • Update the necessary information that you need to update.
  1. Change Password
    • Current Password: Enter your current password that you are using to login to your account.
    • New Password: Enter the new password that you wish to use and which must be minimum of 6 characters and maximum of any characters. Enter the password that you can easily remember but cannot guess by others.
    • Confirm New Password: Once again enter the new password that you have given in New Password field for verification.
  1. Click button will display "Updated!" message.
