Your Shopping Cart

Your Shopping Cart plays an important role in ZeusCart 2.3. It helps user to check out the products. The list of products will be added in this page from various pages like product detail page, recently viewed list, newly listed products and much more. User has privilege to update/remove the products available in shopping cart. From the Shopping Cart page, user has the facility to continue their shopping in ZeusCart 2.3. User can check out the product from this single page and can enter billing address, shipping address and payment gateways to check out the product. You can add the products to shopping cart even without login. But the added products will be removed once you close ZeusCart 2.3 site.

 

  1. Find the product using Search or by browsing.
  2. Click on the image or name of product will get you to Product Detail Page to know the complete information of that product.
  3. Click on button will add the product to cart and opens "Your Shopping Cart" page to view the list of products added in shopping cart.
    • The products will be displayed in a grid with the information of Product Name and Image, Unit Price, Quantity and Sub Total.

 

 

    • Click on the Product Image or Name link will direct you to "Product Detail Page" to view the entire description of product.
    • Add to wishlist: Enable the checkbox and click on button will add the product in your wishlist. You can view your Wishlist by clicking My Wishlist link available in header.
    • Tier Price: Admin may add tier price for the product. If you click on the image of product you will be directed to selected product’s detail page. In detail page, you can view the tier price in "Tier Price" section.
    1. For Example: Suppose the selected Product’s detail page lists the price as $31 - $18 and its details are listed in Tier Price section as
    • Buy 5 for $21.11 each and save 33.3%
    • Buy 8 for $18.11 each and save 41.1%
        • Open "Your Shopping Cart" page and view product’s "Qty" field,
        • If you select between 5 and 7 from the list box and Click button, then its Unit Price will be taken as $21 and its respective Sub Total field displays price of product based on the selected Qty and it displays "Shopping Cart has been Updated Successfully" message.
        • If you select between 1 and 4 and Click button, the Unit Price will be taken as $31 and its respective Sub Total field displays price of product based on the selected Qty and it displays "Shopping Cart has been Updated Successfully" message.
        • If you select 8 and Click button, the Unit Price will be taken as $18 and its respective Sub Total field displays price of product based on the selected Qty and it displays "Shopping Cart has been Updated Successfully" message.
      1. The grid displays the total amount for all products prices, total shipping amount for all products and the grand total of both the total amount of price and shipping cost.
      2. When a product has the Tier Price and you are viewing the product based on quantity then its Original Price will be displayed in Red color with a small strike out in it.

 

 

    • Click button will direct you to the home page of ZeusCart 2.3 and from there you can browse your favorite products.
    • Click icon provided will delete the product from your shopping cart page. You can use this to remove the product which is not needed for checkout process.

The major benefit is the products will not be removed from shopping cart only if the user removes it by clicking “Remove” link

    • Click checkout button will help you to checkout for the product.
