ZeusCart 2.3 Home Page | Right Navigation

 

Right Navigation

The Right Navigation of home page is used to view the number of products listed in shopping cart. It displays the products that are selected for comparison. It has the special place to display products that are in wishlist. The products that are added recently will be listed in the New Products area.

 

 

  • View Cart: This panel helps user to view the total number of products available in their shopping cart and the total number of amount for all products. Users can checkout products from there itself. It displays the products only after the user has logged in with their username and password.
    • Click "COMPARE" button will direct you to the "Compare Products" page to view the products listed with their comparison.
    • To delete all the products added to compare, click "CLEAR ALL" button will display "You have no items to compare" message.
  • Compare Products: When user clicks "Add to Compare" button from pages like products detail page and so on, the products will be added here. Then click on "Compare" button will list the products to compare with all its information. Click "Clear All" will delete all the products available in Compare Products section.
  • New Products: The products that are added newly or recently will be displayed here. It will display the products randomly based on the products added before 15 days. The products will be displayed with their images, name, ratings given and price.
    • Click on the name or image of the product to view the complete details of product.
    • Click Quick Info button to view the short description of product.
    • Click More... link will get you to "All New Products" page where you can view all the new products added and in that page we have provided paging to view all new products. Click on the page numbers to go to corresponding pages and click and to go back and to go next to the page where you are now. By default Eight products will be listed in a page. Click to feed the products in RSS Subscription.
  • Popular Tags: The keywords that are searched most frequently will be displayed. The most frequently searched keyword will be displayed in Large font size and the lowest searched keyword will be displayed in Small font. Clicking on keyword you can view the list of products available based on that keyword. By default, 50 tags will be displayed.
