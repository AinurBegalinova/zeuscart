Getting Inside of ZeusCart 2.3 | Logging in

 

Logging in

If you've already registered, you can Login to your ZeusCart 2.3 account.

 

To login to your ZeusCart 2.3 account:

  1. Go to Home Page of ZeusCart, Click "Login" link provided in the top right, it will get you to Member Login page.

 

 

  1. The Member Login page has two sections as Left and Right.
    • In Left Section i.e., in Shop as a Guest or Register,
      • Shop as Guest: Click on this link will help you go Home Page and continue browsing of products to checkout.
      • Register: Click this link to get Register with us for future convenience of easy and fast shopping.
    • In Right Section i.e., in Login,
      • E-mail Address: Enter the mail id that you have provided while registration.
      • Password: Enter the password that you have given.
      • Enter code: As displaying in the figure you should enter the code in the text box. You can change the code by clicking "Try another one" link.
      • Remember Login: Enable the check box to remember you login in the system forever.
      • Click the button, then you will directed to your Account Dashboard.

 

Forgot your password?

If you have forgotten your password, click on "Forgot your password?" link, to retrieve your password.

 

 

  1. Enter the mail id that you have given in your registration.
  2. Click the "Send My Password" button.
  3. Then the password will be sent to your mail id automatically.
