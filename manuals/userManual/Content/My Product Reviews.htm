My Product Reviews

ZeusCart 2.3 provides you a major opportunity to view the review details of products for which you have reviewed. You can view the complete review details like product name for which you have reviewed, summary of review, ratings you have given for that product and the status of review. After you have reviewed any product from its Details Page, the added review will be displayed in site only after verified by Admin. If he changed the status in to active then it will be displayed in site else it will not displayed.

 

  1. Login to your account using Email Address and Password. Then you will be getting in to Account Dashboard page.
  2. Click "My Product Reviews" link available in the left column on My Account section will open "My Product Reviews" page.
    • The list of products that are reviewed will be displayed in a grid with the information of Date in which you have reviewed the product, Name of product for which you have reviewed, Rating of product and the Status of Review whether it is Active or Inactive.

 

 

After you have reviewed any product from its Details Page, the added review will be displayed in site only after verified by Admin. If he changed the status in to Active then it will be displayed in site else it will not displayed in site and also its status will be In Active.
