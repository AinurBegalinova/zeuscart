Browse by Category

Products on ZeusCart 2.3 are organized into hundreds of different categories and subcategories. To browse by category, choose or click a category that interests you.

 

  1. Select any Category that is listed in the left column of home page.
  2. It displays the Sub Categories that are available under selected main category.

 

 

    • From the Sub Category page you can
      • View Featured Products that are available for that Category.
      • View the bread crumb i.e., from which page you have came to this page.
      • Subscribe for Newsletter, view the list of recently viewed products, view the list of products added in your cart, compare the products, view the list of newly added products, view the popular tags.
  1. Click on any of Sub Category will lists the Products that are available under the selected category and sub category. From the Product page you can

 

 

    • Perform Narrow Search based on Price, Brand and Color of products.
    • View the list of products added in your cart, compare the products, view the list of newly added products, view the popular tags.
    • Click on the name of product to view Product Details page.
    • Add the particular product to Cart, Wishlist and Compare.

If the landing content is added by Admin for your selected product, then the added Landing content will be displayed above the list of products
