Newsletter Subscription

We have provided a separate page to know the status of Newsletter subscription easily. You can subscribe or unsubscribe for the newsletters whenever necessary. If you have subscribed for newsletter the you will be receiving mails from Admin whenever he send the newsletter. Admin may send the newsletter mails to users regarding any important news or announcing any offers and much more. Admin will be sending you newsletter to the Email Address which you have given while registration.

 

  1. Login to your account using Email Address and Password. Then you will be getting in to Account Dashboard page.
  2. Click "Newsletter Subscriptions" link available in the left column on My Account section will open "Newsletter Subscriptions" page.

 

 

    • Enable General Subscription Check box to receive Newsletter mail from Admin else do not check it.
